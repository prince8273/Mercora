# Complex Query Examples for Query Intelligence

A reference list of queries to test the full orchestration pipeline.
Organized by agent type and complexity.

---

## Multi-Agent Deep Analysis
*Triggers all 3 agents (Pricing + Sentiment + Demand Forecast) in DEEP mode*

- A competitor just slashed their prices 30% — analyze our pricing exposure, what customers are saying about us vs them, and forecast the demand impact
- Which single product should I focus on to maximize revenue — factor in pricing, reviews, and demand
- I'm losing market share in electronics — show me pricing gaps, sentiment issues, and demand trends together
- Give me a risk report: which products are overpriced, poorly reviewed, AND have falling demand?
- What's my biggest opportunity right now — cross-analyze pricing, customer feedback, and sales forecast
- A competitor just cut prices — what's the best response?
- How exposed are we to a price war with competitors?
- What should I do to double revenue this quarter?
- Where am I strong and where am I weak across my catalog?
- Why is revenue dropping and what's the fastest lever to pull?
- What is wrong with my business right now?
- Give me a complete report on overall performance
- Analyze pricing, sentiment, and forecast for all products

## Mixed Queries — Pricing + Sentiment

- Our prices are higher than competitors — are customers complaining about it in reviews?
- Which products have both a price gap and negative sentiment?
- Competitor prices dropped 20% — what do customers think about our value for money?
- Are customers leaving bad reviews because of pricing? Show me the data.

---

## Mixed Queries — Pricing + Forecast

- If I lower prices by 15%, what's the expected demand impact next quarter?
- Which products are overpriced AND have declining demand forecasts?
- Show me price gaps and predict how fixing them affects sales next 30 days
- Where should I cut prices to prevent stockouts based on demand trends?

---

## Mixed Queries — Sentiment + Forecast

- Products with the worst reviews — are they also showing declining demand?
- Which products have improving sentiment but low demand forecast — why the disconnect?
- Show me complaint patterns and predict if they'll hurt sales next month
- Customers are requesting a feature — how much demand uplift could that drive?

## Sales + Sentiment Cross-Analysis

- Show me revenue by category alongside customer sentiment scores for each
- Which high-revenue products have surprisingly bad reviews — that's a churn risk

## Sales + Pricing + Forecast (Full Business View)
