# Mercora — E-commerce Intelligence Platform

Mercora is an AI-powered decision intelligence platform for e-commerce teams. It provides actionable business insights through three specialized AI agents:

- **Dhana** — Pricing strategist: competitor monitoring, margin optimization, auto-repricing
- **Vivek** — Sentiment analyst: customer review analysis, theme extraction, insights
- **Agrim** — Demand forecaster: inventory planning, seasonality detection, reorder recommendations

Users interact via natural language queries. The system routes queries to the appropriate agent(s), executes analysis in parallel, and returns structured reports with confidence scores and recommended actions.

The platform supports multi-tenancy — all data is isolated per tenant. It also supports CSV data uploads, scheduled data ingestion from marketplaces/web scraping, and real-time updates via WebSocket.

Target users are e-commerce business owners and analysts who need pricing, sentiment, and demand intelligence without writing code.
